INFORMATION ABOUT DIOR

DChristian Dior S.A (Dior) - a famous luxury company in France was founded on December 16, 1946 at the private house of Christian Dior at 30 Avenue Montaigne Paris B. However, it was not until 1947 that the brand was officially established. The company was founded by Christian Dior but is now run by billionaire Bernard Arnault. Dior is a company specializing in designing and owning a variety of retail stores with many products such as: Fashion, perfume or cosmetics... The customers that Dior targets are women. However, in recent years Dior has expanded its products to include men and children.
