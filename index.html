INFORMATION ABOUT BURBERRY

Burberry is a British luxury fashion house, distributing exclusive luxury sportswear, fashion accessories, fragrances, sunglasses, and cosmetics. The fashion house's signature square stripe pattern has become one of the most widely copied brands.

The Burberry brand logo was officially launched in 1901. This is a logo with historical values as well as the spirit of the times with the image of a horseman holding a spear moving forward in a powerful manner. On top of the spear, there is a flag with the Latin word "Prosum" raised high, meaning "forward".

The Burberry brand was first born in the form of a small store when Founder Thomas Burberry was only 21 years old in the town of Basingstoke, south central England.

The Burberry brand operates in a variety of fashion fields with product lines for men, women, children, accessories, fragrances, cosmetics, and shoes. Burberry watch brand is also one of the product lines that received a lot of attention when launched.
